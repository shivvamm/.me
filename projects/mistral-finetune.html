Case Study · 06 / 07 DDP PyTorch 4×H100

Mistral-7B
on 4×H100.

Fine-tuned Mistral-7B with Distributed Data Parallel across four H100s — cut training time 3× while boosting domain accuracy.

Before · single GPU6 days
After · 4×H100 DDP · 3× faster2 days
01 / Problem

The context.

Fine-tuning a 7B base model on a domain-specific corpus was taking 6 days per cycle on a single GPU — too slow to iterate on data recipes, too expensive to run ablations.

Goal: compress the training cycle to 2 days by moving to distributed training on the team's 4×H100 cluster, without losing reproducibility or stability.

02 / Solution

DDP + careful plumbing.

01
Distributed Data Parallel

PyTorch DDP across 4×H100. Gradient sync, NCCL tuning, mixed-precision. Near-linear scaling on our batch sizes.

02
Optimizer + Memory

Gradient checkpointing + flash-attention + bf16 to fit larger effective batches and keep H100s fed.

03
Reproducibility

Deterministic seeds, checkpoint sharding, and evaluation harness run per-epoch to catch regressions early.
