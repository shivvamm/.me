01 / Problem

The context.

Most complaint intake systems require a human on the other side of the line. That's expensive at volume, slow at night, and painful for customers who just want their issue on record.

Goal: a fully autonomous voice-to-ticket pipeline — a customer calls, speaks their problem, and hangs up with a confirmed case ID. No wait-time, no queue.

02 / Solution

Voice → action.

01
Speech Intake

Whisper v3 transcribes the call with robust handling of accents and background noise.

02
Reasoning

GPT-4 classifies, extracts structured fields (category, severity, contact), and plans dispatch actions.

03
Dispatch

TwilioML executes the plan — ticket created, SMS confirmation, callback scheduled if needed.

03 / My Contributions

What I built.

A.
Integrated Whisper + GPT-4 + Twilio

Streaming STT, reasoning pass, and dispatch orchestration into a single autonomous pipeline.

B.
End-to-end task execution

Zero human-in-loop: from raw audio in to structured case-ID out + SMS confirmation to the caller.
