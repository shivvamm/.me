Case Study · 07 / 07 Knowledge Graph Neo4j OCR

Document
Intelligence.

AI-driven document processing with Neo4j knowledge graphs — 50% reduction in manual processing time and intuitive data exploration.

01 / Problem

The context.

Teams routinely drown in PDFs, scans, and semi-structured documents. Traditional OCR gets you text, but not meaning. To query "which contracts reference Clause 14 and expire in Q3?" you need relationships, not just words.

Goal: turn a doc pile into a navigable knowledge graph so analysts can explore entities and relationships instead of grepping PDFs.

02 / Solution

From PDFs to graphs.

01
OCR + Extraction

Scanned & native PDFs parsed with layout-aware OCR. LLMs extract named entities, clauses, and dates into a typed schema.

02
Graph Construction

Neo4j nodes & relationships generated from structured output with deduplication and confidence scoring.

03
Exploration UI

Analysts query the graph with natural language → Cypher translation, with drilldown into source docs.
